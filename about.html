About AuraBand

Founded 5 years ago by Jiya Sri, AuraBand started with a mission: to make safety effortless and stylish. Our wristband uses AI-driven distress detection, heartbeat & motion sensing, and automatic SOS alerts to protect people in real situations.

Mission

To empower people with a discreet, reliable wearable that detects danger and calls for help — without requiring the user to press a button.

Vision

To make the world safer by combining elegant design with life-saving intelligence.
